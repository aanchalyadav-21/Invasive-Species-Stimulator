Result Summary

The comparison shows that the invasive species spread much faster when no intervention was applied. After introducing control zones, the infestation curve increased more slowly, which reduced the final affected habitat area and improved containment over time.

Without intervention, infestation rose continuously and reached a high final percentage.
With intervention, the spread curve stayed lower throughout the simulation timeline.
This indicates that early action can significantly reduce ecological damage.
