Invasive Species Spread Simulator

A grid-based ecological simulation that models how invasive species spread across habitat cells over time and how early intervention can help reduce ecosystem damage.

Model Overview:
This simulator demonstrates probabilistic spread in a habitat grid. Users can place invasive seed cells and defensive control zones, then observe monthly spread behavior and infestation percentage.
